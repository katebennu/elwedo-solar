ENERGY USAGE AND SAVINGS

Graph showing the energy usage of the households.

Selecting the green buttons you will see different data sets:
Building: compares data of your apartment and of the whole building.
Savings: shows saved energy thanks to the use of solar energy.
Electric car: shows estimated enrgy usage if the family owns an electric car.

Selecting the top blue buttons you will change the scope of the data between the previous day, week or month.
